Health Information Technology Prevalidation Programs

NCQA offers several health information technology (health IT) prevalidation programs. These programs help organizations demonstrate product functionality supporting NCQA evaluation standards.

  For Population Health Management Health IT Solutions 

Population Health Management Prevalidation is for organizations with health IT solutions that support these functions:

Data Integration.

Population assessment.

Segmentation.

Case management systems.

  For PCMH and PCSP Health IT Solutions 

PCMH and PCSP Prevalidations are for organizations with health IT solutions that support these areas:

Team-based care.

Understanding and management of patients.

Access and communication.

Care management.

Care Coordination.

Performance measurement and improvement.
