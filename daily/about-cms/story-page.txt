Reducing Opioid Misuse 

We're responding to the opioid epidemic by promoting safe and responsible pain management, making sure patients can access treatment for opioid use disorder, and using data to target prevention and treatment.

See our roadmap.

Learn how we're fighting the opioid crisis.

*New* Read our RFI and let us know by 5pm ET on 10/11/19 how we can address the opioid crisis with our Action Plan.

Prevention

Stop improper prescribing

Enhance diagnosis of opioid use disorder

Encourage non-opioid pain treatments

Treatment

Make sure people can access treatment

Give patients choices

Support innovation

Data

Understand opioid use patterns

Promote data sharing

Watch trends to see if solutions are working
