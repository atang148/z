  Patient-Centered Recognition Programs 

Prepare for Patient-Centered Recognition Programs by attending NCQA webinars and seminars on program requirements and the application process.

Recognized practices and consultants learn how to sustain NCQA Recognition by attending advanced webinars and seminars.

  PCMH Certified Content Experts 

Attend quarterly live webinars PCMH CCEs to stay current with the latest updates to the program and earn maintenance of certification credit.

  HEDIS 

As the developer of HEDIS®, NCQA offers training on:

Collecting HEDIS data.

HEDIS annual updates.

HEDIS measures.

Best practices for improving HEDIS results.

  Health Plan Accreditation 

Learn how to measure, analyze, report and improve health care quality.

Courses prepare quality improvement staff for the NCQA

Accreditation process, including:

Program standards.

The survey process.

Documentation preparation.
