The Digital Measurement Community

The Digital Measurement Community

NCQA will launch a Digital Measurement Community in 2020.

Members of this dynamic community will:

Interact online with other stakeholders to share knowledge, challenges and experiences.

Learn about the latest strategies and best practices.

Post their own unique perspectives, and;

Help to shape the future of digital measurement.

Your participation grants early exposure to digital measures before they are implemented in HEDIS®. It provides an opportunity to share your organization’s exciting quality improvement work. You’ll also access critical resources for digital measure development and uptake.

Who is eligible to join? A variety of stakeholders including:

Health care innovators

Data intermediaries

Quality measurement experts

Clinical metrics staff

Clinical informatics professionals

Federal and state representatives

CMOs, CMIOs, CIOs

Senior health care decision makers

Quality improvement managers and directors

Clinical data analysts and scientists

Health IT professionals

INTERESTED IN JOINING?

We’re recruiting participants now.

Join the Community mailing list

While we set up the official community platform, you’ll get the latest news and updates. If you need more information or have questions, please  contact the Digital Measurement Community Team at digital.measures@ncqa.org. Please include your organization’s name and contact information.

Exciting quality measurement innovation is ahead. Don’t you want to be a part of it?
